Loading animation

This loading animation is made with css animations and keyframes, it is simply implemented with two divs, one for the "outline" and the other one for the "fill", we increased the width of the fill div inside starting from 0% to 100%.

Bouncing ball

This ball animation is implemented by manipulating with y position of the ball through time,we used several keyframe selectors to change the y position with translateY() function in css.

Heartbeat

For this one we used a mix of pseudo-classes and animations in css, by creating a div with content added before and after to realise the shape of the heart, we of course had to rotate the div to position it perfectly, and then we changed the scale() of the div in three keyframe selectors, at 0%, 50% and 100%.

Fading text
